# Little Saigon Super Market

A static website for Little Saigon Super Market (formerly Ha Tien), located in Saint Paul, MN. The site showcases the store's deli food, groceries, fresh products, and services.

## Pages

- `index.html` ‚Äî Homepage with sections for About, Features, Deli, Groceries, Fresh Product, Services, and Contact
- `deli.html` ‚Äî Full deli food menu
- `groceries.html` ‚Äî Groceries categories
- `freshProduct.html` ‚Äî Fresh product categories
- `services.html` ‚Äî Store services
- `snacks.html`, `tea.html`, `seasoning.html` ‚Äî Individual grocery subcategories
- `vegetables.html`, `freshMeat.html`, `frozenProduct.html` ‚Äî Individual fresh product subcategories
- `sendMoney.html`, `sendPackages.html` ‚Äî Individual service detail pages
