ABOUT US

Once called Ha Tien, now changed to Little Saigon Super Market! Just because we have a different name doesn't mean that we've changed. We still offer the amazing quality Banh Mi that you know and love! We continue to serve delicious deli food at an affordable price. Not only that, our super market has a great selection of oriental goods as well. We offer a wide selection ranging from sauces, meat, produce and much more!
